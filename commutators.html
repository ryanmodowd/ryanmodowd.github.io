Commutators

Info

            Commutators really aren't a method for solving the rubiks cube in and of themselves, but are rather tools that allow the solver to create their own algorithms on the fly. If you've ever made a small mistake in the solving process and end up with 2 corners rotated incorrectly, you may be completely stuck, but with a good knowledge of how to utilize commutators it's an easy fix. Additionally, many cubers spend endless amounts of time memorizing massive amounts of algorithms without ever trying to understand how they work, and commutators give a lot of insight into how a rubiks cube actually works.

Opinion

            Commutators are extremely insightful into the mechanics of a rubiks cube. In my opinion every good speed solver should spend the time to learn about commutators, since they teach you so much. With a strong knowledge of commutators, a rubiks cube can even be solved without any "algorithmic" method (though it would be highly inefficient).
