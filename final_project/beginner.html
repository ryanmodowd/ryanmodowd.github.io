(Recommended Learning Method)

Info

            The beginner's method is the recommended learning method. It doesn't give a lot of insight into the mechinations of the rubik's cube, but for those who just want to learn how to solve the rubik's cube, it's the easiest method to learn. This method is actually the same method that the Rubik's brand teaches on their website, and in all their Rubik's Cube packages.

Opinion

            The beginner's method is definitely a great method to start learning about a rubik's cube, though for those getting more serious about reducing their solving times, the best way is to learn a more effecient method. The beginner's method is less than efficient, and no speedcuber would even consider using it in a competition.
