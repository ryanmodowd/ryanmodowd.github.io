Info

            The corners method is a quirky method in which all of the corner pieces of the rubiks cube are solved first, followed by the edges. This method takes a bit more effort than the beginner's method conceptually, but overall is not much harder to learn. The major difference is that the cube is no longer solved layer by layer, but rather by edge reduction.

Opinion

            The corner's method is not an optimal speedcubing method, but is highly insightful to understanding how a rubiks cube operates. I recommend learning it if you care more about understanding a rubiks cube, though if speed is your only goal, the fridrichs, redkb, or roux method are better choices.
