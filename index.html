About

            The aim of this website is to compile my knowledge and opinions of various rubik's cube methods and ideas with an aesthetically pleasing design, and to share these methods and ideas with others. Solving a rubik's cube is a difficult task that requires effort and commitment, but the learning process is such a rewarding experience. My hope is to give those who are interested in having that experience for themselves a place to learn.
